Bathroom Renovation Project

Bathroom Renovation Project

Planning and Design

We started by assessing the current layout and identifying areas for improvement. After researching trends and styles, we opted for a modern minimalist design, focusing on clean lines and neutral tones.


Demolition and Preparation

The old fixtures, tiles, and cabinetry were removed to make way for the new design. Proper preparation, such as waterproofing and leveling the floors, was essential to ensure durability.


Installing New Fixtures

We chose energy-efficient fixtures, including a dual-flush toilet, a rainfall showerhead, and an LED-lit vanity mirror. These updates not only improved functionality but also added a touch of luxury.


Tiling and Flooring

Large porcelain tiles were installed on the walls and floors, providing a seamless and easy-to-clean surface. The color palette of light gray and white enhanced the sense of space.


Lighting and Ventilation

Adequate lighting was achieved with a combination of recessed ceiling lights and a dimmable pendant over the sink. We also upgraded the ventilation system to prevent mold and maintain air quality.


Finishing Touches

Accessories such as a heated towel rack, floating shelves, and plants added comfort and personality. Finally, we completed the renovation by sealing the surfaces and ensuring everything was functioning perfectly.
